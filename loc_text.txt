What to Know Before You Optimise Local Search                           What is Local SEO?                   Local SEO improves how your business appears when people search for services, products, or businesses in a specific location or nearby area.                           What does Local SEO include?                   It can include Google Business Profile optimisation, maps visibility, local keyword research, citation management, NAP consistency, review strategy, local pages, and location-led content.                           Why is Google Business Profile important?                   For many local searches, the Google Business Profile is the first thing customers see. It helps them check your location, timings, contact details, reviews, photos, services, and directions.                           What is NAP consistency?                   NAP means name, address, and phone number. Consistent NAP information helps customers and search engines trust that your business details are accurate.                           Can Local SEO help with map visibility?                   Yes. Local SEO strengthens the signals that influence how your business appears across map results, local listings, and location-led searches.                           Why do reviews matter for Local SEO?                   Reviews help customers judge trust, recency, quality, and reliability. They also support stronger local credibility when managed consistently.                           Do local pages matter if I already have a Google Business Profile?                   Yes. Local pages help explain your services, service areas, branches, and location relevance in more detail than a profile can.                           How do you measure Local SEO success?                   We look at local rankings, Google Business Profile actions, calls, direction requests, website visits, enquiries, review movement, and location-led traffic.                           Is Local SEO useful for multi-location businesses?                   Yes. Multi-location businesses need consistent local visibility, location-specific pages, accurate listings, review systems, and reporting across every service area.                           Is Local SEO right for every business?                It is most useful when customers choose based on location, proximity, reviews, trust, convenience, or service-area relevance.
