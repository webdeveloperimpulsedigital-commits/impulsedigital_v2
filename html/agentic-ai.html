What to Know Before AI Starts Working for You

What is Agentic AI?

+

Agentic AI refers to AI systems that can understand context, take action, use connected tools, and move workflows forward within approved boundaries.

How is Agentic AI different from a chatbot?

+

A chatbot replies. Agentic AI can reply, qualify, follow up, update systems, trigger actions, and escalate to humans when needed.

What workflows can Agentic AI support?

+

Lead qualification, customer support, appointment booking, campaign responses, reactivation journeys, CRM updates, document collection, reminders, and workflow routing.

Can AI agents work across WhatsApp, email, voice, DMs, and SMS?

+

Yes. Agents can work across WhatsApp, email, voice notes, Facebook Messenger, Instagram DM, SMS, website chat, and CRM workflows, depending on your setup.

How do you control accuracy and brand voice?

+

We define approved information, tone, response rules, escalation logic, and review processes before the agent goes live.

When does a human step in?

+

A human steps in when a conversation is complex, sensitive, high-value, uncertain, or outside the agent's approved scope.

What KPIs should we track?

+

Response time, qualification rate, follow-up completion, meeting bookings, resolution rate, handoff rate, conversion movement, manual hours saved, and cost per resolved interaction.

What should not be automated?

+

Anything that requires sensitive judgment, legal interpretation, complex negotiation, emotional handling, or high-risk decision-making should remain human-led or require human approval.
