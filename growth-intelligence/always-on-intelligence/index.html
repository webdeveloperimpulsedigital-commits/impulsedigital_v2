What is Always-On Intelligence?

+

It is a continuous monitoring system that tracks brand mentions, category movement, competitor activity, reviews, and reputation signals across digital platforms to help teams act early.

How is this different from standard social listening?

+

Standard listening often provides raw dashboard metrics. Always-On Intelligence filters out noise, connects multiple platforms (social, news, e-commerce), and adds human interpretation so the output is decision-ready.

What decisions can this support?

+

It can support crisis management, campaign tracking, e-commerce optimisation, brand health monitoring, leadership reporting, and rapid response planning.

What is Reputation and Crisis Tracking?

+

It is a specialized layer of monitoring designed to catch negative sentiment, complaint spikes, and risky conversations before they escalate into larger brand issues.

What is E-Commerce and Review Intelligence?

+

It tracks product feedback, reviews, ratings, and sentiment across marketplaces, helping teams understand what drives or blocks conversion at the point of sale.

What do we receive?

+

Outputs depend on the rhythm you need—ranging from live dashboards and instant risk alerts to weekly summaries, monthly insight reports, and quarterly deep dives.

Who should use this?

+

Brand teams, corporate communications, digital marketing, e-commerce managers, and leadership teams who need real-time visibility into brand health and market risks.
