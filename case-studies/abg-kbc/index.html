ABG x KBC

Introducing a new purpose through a moment
India already trusted.

Aditya
Birla
Group
was
moving
from
"Big
in
Your
Life"
to
"A
Force
for
Good."

That
shift
needed
more
than
a
new
line.

It
needed
a
moment
people
could
understand
quickly,
believe
instinctively,
and
remember
after
the
campaign
had
passed.

The
Outcome

4.3Mn+
TV reach
1Mn+

1
trusted national platform
1
purpose made visible

The

A

when

For
ABG,
the

to
introduce
A
Force
for
Good

with

The

The

It

human.
It
had

It

without

a

One

One
child's
education.
One
purpose
made
visible.

What

to

The

used

to

Every

It

A

And

could

Impulse

  • Digital and television-ready creative assets

  • Real-time coordination across stakeholders for rollout
  • Consistent translation of the Force for Good narrative across touchpoints

By
