The

It

in

to

Reach

The
campaign
had
to
solve
three

Reach
consumers
within
selected
geo-locations.
Encourage
online
coupon

Support
offline
store
redemption.

Broad
reach

The
campaign
needed
targeting
sharp

messaging
flexible

to

What
needed
to
change

The
campaign
could
not
treat
every
consumer,
location,
and
product
the

Premium
products
needed
more

Non-premium
products
needed

mass

Both

same
coupon
journey
without
friction.

  • Location
  • Product category
  • Consumer behaviour
  • Landing page action
  • Backend coupon delivery

What
Impulse
worked
on

Impulse
structured
the
campaign
around
local
relevance
and
action.

  • Facebook and Instagram campaign planning
  • 3 km radius targeting
  • Audience behaviour inputs
  • Geo-targeting across Pune and Delhi
  • Product bifurcation across premium and non-premium categories
  • Landing page flow for user detail capture
  • Phone number authentication
  • Backend transfer to HUL
  • Instant SMS coupon delivery

The
goal
was

make
the
journey
easy
for

user
and

campaign.

The
Coupon
Path

The
user
journey
had
to
keep
the
next
step
obvious.

01 / LAND
A consumer landed on the campaign page.
02 / DETAILS
Entered their details.
03 / VERIFY
Verified their phone number.
04 / TRANSFER
The data moved to HUL's backend.
05 / COUPON
An SMS coupon was delivered instantly.
06 / ACTION
The user had a clear path from digital intent to offline redemption.
